Physics Model: Realistic Motion

How conservation of energy creates authentic roller coaster physics through mathematical modeling.

Conservation of Energy

$$mgh_{start} = mgh + \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$

Calculated Velocity v(x)

Master Equation (Updated Constants):

$$v(x) = \sqrt{2g(20.0 - f(x))}$$

G-Forces and Centripetal Acceleration

The force felt by the rider depends on the speed and the tightness of the curve.

Centripetal Acceleration ($a_c$):

$$a_c = \frac{v^2}{\rho} = v^2 \cdot \kappa$$

This explains why high speed ($v$) combined with high curvature ($\kappa$) creates the strongest forces.

Total G-Force Equation:

$$Gs(x) = \frac{1 + [f'(x)]^2 + 2(h_{start} - f(x)) \cdot f''(x)}{(1 + [f'(x)]^2)^{3/2}}$$
